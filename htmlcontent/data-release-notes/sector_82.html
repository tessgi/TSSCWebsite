    Sector Notes

    Noted Issue Description
    Spacecraft pointing: The pointing in Sector 82 was set at +55.5 degrees in ecliptic latitude. Camera 4 was pointed at the North Ecliptic Pole. Cameras 1 and 4 were used for guiding in all four orbit segments: Orbits 171 a, b and 172 a, b.
    Scattered light: In Sector 82, the Earth introduces scattered light signals in Camera 4 towards the middle of each orbit, and the Moon impacts Cameras 1 near the middle of Orbit 171b.
