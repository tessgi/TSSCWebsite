    Sector Notes

    Noted Issue Description
    Spacecraft pointing: The pointing in Sector 92 was set at 0.7 degrees in ecliptic latitude. The four science cameras were oriented along the Ecliptic plane. Camera 1 was used for guiding in Orbits 191a and 192a. Camera 4 was used for guiding in Orbits 191a, 191b, and 192b.
    Scattered light: In Sector 92, the Earth introduces scattered light signals in all Cameras towards the beginning and end of each orbits. The Moon introduces a signal in all Cameras at the end of orbit 191 and start of 192.
