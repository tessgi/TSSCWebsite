    Sector Notes

    Noted Issue Description
    Reverse-clocked CCD data: Reverse-clocked CCD data were collected in an annual assessment of the health of the TESS CCDs. This occured between 2025-03-25 - 2025-03-26 (cadences 1823600 - 1823960).
    Spacecraft pointing: The pointing in Sector 90 was set at -55.5 degrees in ecliptic latitude. Camera 4 was pointed near the South Ecliptic Pole. Cameras 1 and 4 were used for guiding in orbit segments 187 a, 187 b, and 188 a. Camera 4 was used for guiding in Orbit 188 b.
    Scattered light: In Sector 90, the Earth introduces scattered light signals in Camera 4 towards the beginning and of each orbit. The Moon impacts Cameras 1 and 2 at the start of Orbit 187a and end of Orbit 188b.
