    Sector Notes

    Noted Issue Description
    Spacecraft pointing: The pointing in Sector 98 was set at -54.0 degrees in ecliptic latitude. Camera 4 was pointed near the South Ecliptic Pole. Camera 1 was used for guiding in Orbits 205b, 206b, 207b and 208b. Camera 4 was also used for guiding in all eight half-orbit segments.
    Scattered light: In Sector 98, the Earth introduces scattered light signals in Camera 1 near the beginning of each orbit. The Moon introduces a signal in Cameras 1 and 2 in orbit 205 and Camera 1 in orbit 207.
