    Sector Notes

    Noted Issue Description
    Spacecraft pointing: The pointing in Sector97 was set at -54.0 degrees in ecliptic latitude. Camera 4 was pointed near the South Ecliptic Pole. Camera 1 was used for guiding in Orbits 201b, 202b, 203b and 204b. Camera 4 was also used for guiding in all eight half-orbit segments.
    Scattered light: In Sector 97, the Earth introduces scattered light signals in Cameras 1, 2, ans 3 towards the beginning of each orbit. The Moon introduces a signal in Camera 1 in orbit 204.
