    Sector Notes

    Noted Issue Description
    Spacecraft pointing: The pointing in Sector 81 was set at +55.5 degrees in ecliptic latitude. Camera 4 was pointed at the North Ecliptic Pole. Camera 4 was used for guiding in all four orbit segments. Camera 1 was also used for guiding in three of the orbit segments (169a, 170a and 170b).
    Scattered light: In Sector 81, the Earth introduces scattered light signals towards the end of each orbit, and the Moon impacts Cameras 1 and 2 near the middle of Orbit 169b.
