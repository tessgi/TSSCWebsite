    Sector Notes

    Noted Issue Description
    Spacecraft pointing: The pointing in Sector 85 was set at 55.5 degrees in ecliptic latitude. Camera 4 was pointed at the North Ecliptic Pole. Cameras 1 and 4 were used in Orbits 177a, 177b, and 178b.
    Scattered light: In Sector 85, the Earth introduces scattered light signals towards the end of each orbit, and the Moon impacts Cameras 1 and 2 near the middle of Orbit 178a.
