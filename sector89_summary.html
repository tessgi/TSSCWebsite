    Sector Notes

    Noted Issue Description
    Spacecraft pointing: The pointing in Sector 89 was set at -55.5 degrees in ecliptic latitude. Camera 4 was pointed near the South Ecliptic Pole. Cameras 1 and 4 were used for guiding in orbit segments 185 a, 185 b and 186 a. Camera 4 was used for guiding in Orbit 186 b.
    Scattered light: In Sector 88, the Earth introduces scattered light signals in Camera 4 towards the beginning end of each orbit, and the Moon impacts Cameras 1 and 2 near the start of Obrit 185a and end or 186b.
